Project name:

Career Speed Dating

Details:

My first major project as a team lead was Career Speed Dating, a program that pairs students with potential employers for quick, 15-minute conversations. I started with a poster concept, then refined it into the final print piece with all key details like date, venue, and a sign-up QR code. The visual language builds on modular paths and keywords like query, connect, interview, and succeed.


Beyond the poster, I designed Instagram posts and stories, including a panorama post, to promote the event. I also prepared headers for our PR team to use in web articles, created a thank you certificate template for sponsors, and proposed merchandise, designing front and back shirt layouts with mockups.
